# Privacy Policy for Mitta - Kalyana Yoga Studio

_Effective Date: 30 Jan 2024_

Thank you for choosing Mitta, the membership and class booking web application provided by Kalyana Yoga Studio. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information. By using Mitta, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

- When you sign up for Mitta, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details.

### 1.2 Authentication Information

- To enhance security and facilitate Single Sign-On (SSO) using Facebook or Google, we collect necessary authentication information from your chosen social media platform.

### 1.3 Email Sign-In

- Alternatively, you can sign in using your email address. In this case, we collect and store the necessary information securely.

### 1.4 Booking and Membership Data

- We collect data related to your class bookings, membership status, and preferences within Mitta to provide you with personalized services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Service Provision

- We use your personal information to provide you with access to Mitta, including class booking features, membership management, and other related services.

### 2.2 Communication

- We may use your contact details to send you important updates, notifications, and information regarding your membership, class bookings, or the Mitta application.

### 2.3 Improvements and Customization

- Your usage data helps us improve Mitta's features and personalize your experience based on your preferences and history.

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 Third-Party Integration

- Mitta integrates with Facebook and Google for Single Sign-On purposes. By using these features, you authorize the sharing of authentication information between Mitta and your chosen social media platform.

### 3.2 Legal Compliance

- We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

### 4.2 SSL Encryption

- Mitta uses SSL encryption to secure data transmission between your device and our servers, ensuring the confidentiality of your information.

## 5. Your Choices

### 5.1 Account Settings

- You can review and update your account settings, including privacy preferences, within Mitta.

### 5.2 Communication Preferences

- You have the option to opt out of non-essential communications from Mitta.

## 6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

### 6.1 Notification

- We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Notification of significant changes will be provided through Mitta or other means.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at aurick@aurickf.com.

Thank you for trusting Mitta and Kalyana Yoga Studio with your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a secure and enjoyable experience.
