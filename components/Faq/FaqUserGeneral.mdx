## General Question

#### I want to share a class information to my friend, how can I do it?

Class information can be shared by clicking share icon <i className="pi pi-share-alt" /> next to the class title in search page or in **My Booking** section in home page.
This class information page is available for public to view.

#### Why my in-app notification message suddenly disappeared?

In-app notification message automatically deleted after certain period of time.
