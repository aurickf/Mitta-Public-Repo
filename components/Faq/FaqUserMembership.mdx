## Membership

#### How long is each membership validity ?

Each membership package might have different validity period. You can check on the request form on your next purchase.

#### What happened if my membership expired ?

You will no longer able to book our class.

However upon purchase of new membership, your existing balance will be carried over.

#### Can I use my existing membership to book special class?

No, your membership can only be used for our regular class. Special class is marked on its upper right corner on search result.
